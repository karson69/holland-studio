About

Hi name is Karson Holland and live in Thunderbay Ontario, currently I am a 2nd year college student in the Interactive Media Development program at Confederation College.
Outside of college I am making a small game with afew of my friends from my program, as well as, making graphic requests for members on a discord server. I enjoy making graphics for other people or myself and am experienced in Photoshop, Illustrator, Audition, Premiere, After Effects, Indesign, Lightroom, 3ds Max, Blender, Unity, Mudbox, Match Mover, C++, and JavaScript.
Im currently employeed at a small bait shop in Kakabeka Falls and have been working there for about 5 years now and am part time helping a chiropractors office with there online needs, I have also worked at many other places like Excalibur Motorcycle Works, Walmart, Kakabeka Baits, and for my dad with his trucking company.
For fun I like to go on hikes and usually take pictures while im out on my adventures, I also play abit of sports like Hockey, Snowbaording, and Volleyball. On my free time I enjoy playing video games with my friends and have competed in acouple small Counterstrike Global Offensive tournaments coming in the top 3 teams but have not won one yet!
After College I am interested in becoming a 3d artist because of how much I love making 3d scenes and objects and having everything come together to make a beautiful interactive enviroment.
